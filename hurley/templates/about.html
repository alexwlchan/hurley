What is this nonsense?

This started as a joke in episode #15 of Cortex, a podcast on the Relay.FM network.

The hosts were joking about the Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon, and how Myke is the Kevin Bacon of the podcasting world. He founded his own podcast network, and co-hosts many of the shows. They were wondering how many connections you could find from Myke to other popular podcasters.

Lots of people were making static charts, but those are hard to follow and don't scale to many podcasters. I made this site as a bit of fun while cooking dinner.

It serves no purpose, it's just a bit of fun. I hope it makes you smile. :-)
